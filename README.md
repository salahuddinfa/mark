## **2. History of the Company**


### (1) Organizational History


<table>
<tr>
<td>Organizational History</td>
<td>The Company was founded in March 1960 in Minato-ku, Tokyo, with the goal of publishing job recruitment advertisements for various companies in university newspapers. At that time, the Company was named "University Newspaper Advertising Company," the predecessor of Recruit Holdings Co., Ltd. Subsequently, the Company entered into a contract to handle advertisements for multiple university newspapers and established University Advertising Co., Ltd. in October 1960 as a corporate organization. In 1962, the Company started publishing "Invitation to Companies." In April 1963, the Company changed its name to Japan Recruit Co., Ltd. In August of the same year, the Company was established as Recruit Co., Ltd. (Please refer to the timeline below for subsequent organizational changes.)</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Business Expansion</td>
<td>The Company started as a specialized advertising agency for university newspapers in 1960. Two years later, it published "Invitation to Companies," which collected job information for university students, establishing a business model called the "Ribbon Model" that connected individual users with corporate clients. In addition to expanding its human resources-related business, including mid-career recruitment, human resource introduction, and temporary staffing, the Company also expanded into areas related to life events such as school, housing, used cars, weddings, and travel, dining, and beauty. In recent years, the Company has expanded its business domain to include services that utilize SaaS (Software as a Service) to support the operations and management of small and medium-sized enterprise clients, including retail and restaurant businesses.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Digitalization of Information</td>
<td>The Company has continuously promoted the digitalization of information, introducing computers in the early days when their use was still rare. Through the digitalization of information, the Company has promoted the speed and efficiency of its business operations. After researching supercomputers in the 1980s, the Company shifted from paper media (information magazines) to the Internet and mobile platforms in the 1990s. In addition to making information available more easily and quickly, the Company developed innovative online reservation management systems and provided overwhelming convenience to individual users and corporate clients through the digitalization of information. The Company is accelerating the expansion of its cloud-based SaaS solutions.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Globalization</td>
<td>The Company began expanding its business into the global market in the 2000s. Initially, it operated a marriage-related business in China but withdrew after a few years. However, this experience was utilized in its subsequent M&amp;A-based overseas business strategy. Following the acquisition of The CSI Companies, Inc. in the United States, the Company accelerated its acquisitions in the temporary staffing business. While working to improve the productivity of the acquired organizations, the Company expanded its business to various countries around the world, including Europe and Australia. The acquisitions of Indeed, Inc. in 2012 and Glassdoor LLC in 2018 added the HR Technology business, driving the growth of the entire Group. The Company's services are now available in over 60 countries and regions.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Evolution as a Matching Platform</td>
<td>The Company has evolved its advertising-based media business into a marketplace business model that provides more optimal matching solutions for both individual users and corporate clients using technology. In the HR Technology and temporary staffing businesses, the Company aims to further provide value to job seekers and companies by simplifying the recruitment process under the "Simplify Hiring" strategy. In the Marketing, Matching & Technology business, the Company aims to realize the productivity and profitability of corporate clients under the "Help Businesses Work Smarter" strategy by building an ecosystem that supports the operation of corporate clients with matching platforms and SaaS. The Company is also focusing on further improving the efficiency and speed of matching by utilizing AI developed through its accumulated experience.</td>
</tr>
</table>
